Meet Yechiel

The Heart of the Farm

A Real Marine Biologist

Yechiel is a marine biologist with nearly 50 years of studying and caring for fish and aquatic creatures. His passion for water life inspires every adventure in these books.

Years of Research & Discovery

At Bar-Ilan University, Yechiel worked in the Fish and Marine Invertebrate Laboratory, studying how fish live and behave in their natural homes.

He also worked at Water Marks, learning about fish ecosystems and water quality—understanding exactly what makes a healthy home for fish and other creatures.

His work at Colors deepened his knowledge of fish behavior and how different species interact with each other and their environment.

Every Day on the Farm

Yechiel tends to his farm with care and patience.

He keeps the water clean and healthy.

He watches how fish behave and learn.

He creates safe, thriving homes for every creature.

Real Stories from Real Science

Every character in this series is inspired by real observations. George the Guppy? Based on real guppy behavior. Marina the Shrimp? Real creature, real personality. Each adventure teaches something true about the amazing aquatic world.

When children understand real science, they can see the magic in nature all around them.

"Every fish deserves a thriving home. When we understand and care for nature, we become part of something wonderful."
