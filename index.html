Meet the Author

Yechiel Kuperman

Marine Biologist | Fishery Expert | Storyteller

✓ 50 Years of Real Fish Stories

After nearly five decades studying aquatic life, Yechiel is bringing the hidden worlds of his Fishery Farm to young readers. Every story is rooted in real observation, real science, and real passion.

From Science to Story: His credentials span some of the world's finest aquaculture operations:

• Bar-Ilan University: Fish and Marine Invertebrate Laboratory research
• Water Marks: One of the world's best gourami and cichlid farms — expertise in breeding, care, and behavior
• Colors Farm: One of the world's finest guppy farms and environmental facility — 50+ years of real guppy knowledge

The Real Expertise Behind the Magic: Yechiel doesn't imagine fish stories — he lives them. From his work with cichlids and gouramis to managing one of the world's best guppy operations, he knows fish behavior, water chemistry, ecology, and animal psychology intimately. Each character reflects real species traits observed across decades. Each plot teaches real science.

Why This Matters for Children: Stories rooted in truth create deeper learning. When children understand that adventure can be real, they develop genuine curiosity about nature, science, and discovery.

"After 50 years with fish, I'm sharing what they've taught me. Every character in these stories swims with purpose. Every lesson is real."
